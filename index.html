Hi! I'm Yaurie.
I'm a junior at Northwestern University studying Computer Science and Learning Sciences. Thanks for stopping by!
You might be wondering what Learning Sciences even is.
Learning Sciences explores cognition and design with an education lens. My classes feel like a mix of psychology, sociology and teacher education, sprinkled with some design and computer science. The major sits within Northwestern's School of Education and Social Policy.
I probably don't need to explain my Computer Science major. Interestingly, Northwestern has a great CS + LS PhD program, so many CS professors are also faculty in LS! This has made my independent research super fascinating yet easy to pursue. Feel free to read more about my research in my Projects section.
Outside of class, I love to volunteer in my community. Equity work and community engagement are really important to me. I also enjoy exploring Chicago, event planning, and making memes for my school's Barstool/Chicks accounts.
