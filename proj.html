Lumi Student Matcher
LumiNUS Tutors (Lumi for short) is a startup organization that provides free mentorship and tutoring to low-income high school students in Chicago. I joined Lumi when it was just an idea. I helped the 3 cofounders develop and complete our first-ever tutor program.

I helped create a program that matches tutors with students using data structures and algorithms on Python (pandas) to automate the pairing process and create the best possible matches.

Perceptions of Professional Programmers
I conducted my own independent research study that investigated how beginner CS (CS1) students perceive professional programmers. Many CS1 students believe that professionals rarely make syntax errors, look up things online, or ask for help, which can affect how they think of themselves as programmers.

I analyzed the quantitative data with guidance from my mentor, Dr. Jamie Gorson. I presented a lightning talk at ICER 2022 and my paper is in press for the SIGCE 2023 Student Competition.
