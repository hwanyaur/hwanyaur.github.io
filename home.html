👋Hi! I'm Yaurie
I'm a junior at Northwestern University studying Computer Science 💻 and Learning Sciences 🧠. Thanks for stopping by!
I probably don't need to explain my Computer Science major, but you might be wondering what Learning Sciences entails 🤔.
Learning Sciences (LS) explores cognition and design with an education lens. My classes feel like a mix of psychology, sociology and teacher education, sprinkled with some design and computer science. Interestingly, Northwestern has a great CS + LS PhD program, so many CS professors are also faculty in LS! Because of this, I've gotten the chance to work in research📈 and even lead my own independent project in my interest areas. Feel free to read more about my research in my Projects section 🤓.
Outside of class, I love to volunteer in my community👥. Equity work and community engagement are really important to me. I also enjoy exploring Chicago🏙, event planning, and helping run my school's Barstool/Chicks accounts. I am currently reading Victor Ray's On Critical Race Theory: Why It Matters and Why You Should Care.
